Dartmouth Society of CS Students

The Dartmouth Society of Computer Science Students (DSOCSS) is a student-run organization that aims to form a strong CS community on campus through team projects and various events.

About Us

Our club began in 2024 with the goal of promoting the study of computer science through a collaborative learning community. We program various projects in small teams to learn technical skills and explore applications of computer science that interest us!

Joining

Our club is open to new members!

If you are a Dartmouth student interested in joining our team, reach out to Arthur Zarins '27. Joining the club early on gives you a great opportunity to meet other CS students and help shape the future of our organization.

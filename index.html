Dartmouth Society of CS Students

The Dartmouth Society of Computer Science Students (DSOCSS) is a student-run organization that aims to form a strong CS community on campus through team projects and various events.

About Us

Founded in 2024, we are beginning our club by doing small team coding projects. Join the team if you are interested!

Joining

We are open to new members any time! To join our team, reach out to Arthur Zarins '27. Joining the club early on gives you a great opportunity to meet other CS students and help shape the future of our organization.

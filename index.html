Dartmouth Society of CS Students

The Dartmouth Society of Computer Science Students (DSOCSS) is a student-run organization that aims to form a strong CS community on campus through team projects and various events.

About Us

Dartmouth CS Society began in January 2024 with the goal of promoting the study of computer science through a collaborative learning community. We program various projects in small teams to learn technical skills and explore applications of computer science that interest us!

Previous Events

Previous events include a private equity hackathon, team coding projects, advent of code competition, technical interview prep, course advising, and social events!

Joining

If you are a Dartmouth student interested in joining CS Society, please reach out to any of our board members! Joining the CS Society gives you a great opportunity to meet other CS students, learn technical skills, and help shape the future of our organization.

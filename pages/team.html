Officers

Joining

We are open to new members any time! Please reach out to any of our officers to learn more information or join our team. Joining the CS Society gives you a great opportunity to meet other CS students and help shape the future of our organization.

Meeting Information

We typically have club meetings once a week. Our meeting topics include coding projects, technical workshops, and social events. DSOCSS Project teams meet individually as needed throughout the week to code and debug together.

Membership

We are open to all skill levels, but we recommend that students take Dartmouth's introductory course, CS 1 before joining our club (or have equivalent knowledge).

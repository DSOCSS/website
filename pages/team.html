Joining

We are open to new members! Please reach out to any of our board members to learn more information or join our club. Joining the CS Society gives you a great opportunity to meet other CS students, learn technical skills, and help shape the future of our organization.

Meeting Information

Meetings are announced in advance via email to our email list and listserv. Our past events include a hackathon, team coding projects, advent of code competition, technical interview prep, course advising, and social events.

Membership

We are open to all skill levels, but we recommend that students take or concurrently enroll in Dartmouth's introductory course, Computer Science 1 before joining our club (or have equivalent knowledge).

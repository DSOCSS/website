Meeting Information

We meet hold general club meetings once a week. Each CS project team meets individually as needed throughout the week to code and debug together.

Joining

We are open to new members any time! To join our team, reach out to Arthur Zarins '27. Joining the club early on gives you a great opportunity to meet other CS students and help shape the future of our organization.

Membership

We are open to all skill levels, but we recommend that students take Dartmouth's introductory course, CS 1 before joining our club (or have equivalent knowledge).
